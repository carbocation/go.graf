{{.G.Site}} is a threaded discussion forum written in Go.

  • Instant New comments are loaded over WebSockets and appear as soon as they are posted.
  • Nested Comments are threaded, to allow for easy identification of which comments are in reply to one another. This fosters discussions that branch off from the main topic.
  • Recursive Post and comments are ranked based not only on the upvotes and downvotes that they have received, but also based on the score of all of of their child comments (recursively down the tree). The hypothesis is that this will surface interesting discussion, even when the discussion is sparked by low-value or even negative-value posts.
  • Responsive {{.G.Site}} is also designed with responsiveness in mind, including upvote and downvote buttons that are much larger and more widely spaced than in the typical news discussion forum.
  • Bcrypted {{.G.Site}} stores bcrypted passwords. If our database is ever compromised, your password is still protected by one of the best password protection mechanisms available. As always, choosing a high-entropy password is a good idea.
