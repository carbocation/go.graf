{{.G.Site}} is a threaded discussion forum written in Go.

  • Recursive {{.G.Site}} ranks each post or comment based not only on the upvotes and downvotes that it has received, but also based on the score of all of its child comments (recursively down the tree). The hypothesis is this will surface interesting discussion, even when the discussion is sparked by low-value or even negative-value posts.
  • Responsive This site is also designed with reasonable amounts of responsiveness in mind. To that end, the upvote and downvote buttons are much larger and more widely spaced than in the typical news discussion forum.
  • Bcrypted This site stores bcrypted passwords. If our database is ever compromised, your password is still protected by one of the best password protection mechanisms available. As always, choosing a high-entropy password is a good idea.
